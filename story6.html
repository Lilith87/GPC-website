My name is Nafas Gul Mohammadi, one of the students deprived of continuing education in Afghanistan. Being excluded from school was a challenging experience that unfortunately limited my access to formal education and personal growth opportunities. However, every day I pursue educational opportunities with a sense of hope within me. Along the way, I used to learn from different local and international online programs. Dealing with this situation is so much difficult for me as it is for all Afghan girls, but I have never give up. I always tried to learn from myself, my experiences, difficulties, and problems I face. I try hard each moment for my dreams, goals, and very importantly for my future. In addition, I use my phone and laptop to help my sisters in Afghanistan by providing educational opportunities, online English classes, sharing my knowledge, and keeping them motivated to try for their goals and have hope. Since 2021 I learned to be strong, I understand how brave I can be. Although I have a broken heart because of my dreams and goals, still I keep myself motivated and strong. One of my biggest hope is being able to access quality education, where I would be able to feel free, relax, and study.

Finally, I want from all “Human Rights Institutions” to not forget Afghan girls and their goals. And my message to Afghan girls is, “you are all very strong, so please never give up, try each moment, and stay motivated because nothing is impossible!”

Nafas Gul Mohammadi

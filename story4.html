I am writing to share with you the unfulfilled dreams I had as an Afghan girl. Growing up, I had aspirations and hopes for a bright future filled with opportunities and possibilities. However, the challenges and obstacles that came my way prevented me from realizing these dreams.

The struggles and hardships faced by Afghan girls, including limited access to education, societal barriers, and lack of support, have hindered the fulfillment of my dreams. Despite these setbacks, I remain resilient and determined to overcome these challenges and strive for a better tomorrow. I believe that by raising awareness, advocating for change, and supporting each other, we can create a world where every girl, regardless of her background, can pursue her dreams and achieve her full potential. Let us work together to break down barriers and create a more inclusive and empowering environment for all girls to thrive.

Sahar Andarabi, Member of GPC

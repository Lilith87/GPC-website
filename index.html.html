    Overview of GPC

    Girls on the Path of Change (GPC) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering Afghan girls and young women by providing access to high-quality education and fostering a supportive community.

    Key programs

    GPC Academy

    Offering English courses and TOEFL preparation to unlock global opportunities

    Youths on the Path of Change (YPC)

    Collaborating with boys to support girls in their educational journey.

    Committees

    Explore our photography, painting, writing, and poetry programs.

    Our impact

    Since our founding, GPC has empowered hundreds of girls with the tools they need to succeed.

Our story

Girls on the Path of Change (GPC) was founded in April 2023 by Robina Azizi, a young 17-year-old girl denied an education by the Taliban. After fleeing Afghanistan, Robina was determined to ensure that girls could still access learning and opportunities. Through GPC, Robina has built a global community providing girls with online classes led by international educators and a safe space to connect and learn. Today, GPC is dedicated to empowering girls denied education to pursue their dreams, offering them education, hope, and a voice for change.

"Our mission is to provide Afghan girls and young women with the skills and confidence to pursue their dreams. We envision a future where every girl has access to the education she needs to succeed"
